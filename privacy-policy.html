Privacy Policy

This web site is owned and operated by APPTUTTi.  At www.apptutti.com, protecting your privacy is priority.  This privacy statement is to demonstrate our firm commitment to privacy security, and to illustrate the processes we use. In case you have any questions regarding this policy, APPTUTTi can be contacted by e-mail at info@apptutti.com .  The following discloses our information gathering and dissemination practices for this site:

Collection of Personal or Corporate Information

APPTUTTi may automatically collect and track:

  1. the home server domain names, email addresses, type of client computer and type of web browser of visitors/users to APPTUTTi web site;
  2. the email addresses of visitors/users that communicate with APPTUTTi via email;
  3. information knowingly provided by the visitor/user in online forms, registration forms, surveys, and contest entries, such as legal name, address, telephone numbers, method of payment, password, and credit card number;
  4. demographic information, such as age, income level, and gender, if provided;
  5. and visitor/user-specific information on what pages visitors/users access.

In addition, APPTUTTi does place Internet “cookies” on visitors/users’ hard drives.  Internet “cookies” are tiny data files that save the visitor/user’s name, password, username, shopping cart (if applicable) and site preferences.  This benefits the visitor/user, because in the future when the visitor/user revisits APPTUTTi web site, APPTUTTi will recognize the visitor/user by the Internet cookie and customize the visitor/user’s experience accordingly.  Internet cookies are stored on the visitor/user’s hard drive and are not stored, in any form, by APPTUTTi.  Visitors/Users may decline the Internet cookie by using the appropriate feature of their web client software, if available.

Use of Personal or Corporate Information

Personal or Corporate Information collected by APPTUTTi will be used by APPTUTTi for editorial and quality assurance purposes, for marketing and promotional purposes, for a statistical analysis of users’ behavior, for product development, for content improvement, to inform advertisers as to how many visitors/users have seen or clicked on their advertisements, and to customize content and layout of APPTUTTi site.  Names, postal and email addresses, and phone numbers collected may be added to APPTUTTi databases and used for future calls and mailings regarding site updates, new products and services, upcoming events, and status of orders placed online.

Opt-Out Right

Visitors/Users may opt out of having their personal or corporate information collected by APPTUTTi, disclosed to third parties, or third parties to send promotional correspondences to the visitors/users, by contacting APPTUTTi via postal mail, email, or telephone, or by checking the appropriate box, if any, on the online form.

Access to and Ability to Correct Personal or Corporate Information

Upon request via postal mail, email, or phone, APPTUTTi will provide visitors/users a summary of any personal or corporate information retained by APPTUTTi regarding the visitor/user.  Visitors/Users may modify, correct, change, or update their personal record or cause their personal record to be removed from APPTUTTi database.  APPTUTTi will only send personal records to the email address on file for the visitor/user name associated with it.

No Liability for Errors in Transmission or for Acts of Third Parties

Although APPTUTTi will use all reasonable efforts to safeguard the confidentiality of the visitor/user-related information collected, transmissions made by means of the Internet cannot be made absolutely 100% secure.  APPTUTTi will have no liability whatsoever for disclosure of visitor/user-related information due to errors in transmission or due to unauthorized acts of third parties.

APPTUTTi Right to Contact User

APPTUTTi reserves the right to contact site visitors/users regarding account status and changes to the privacy policy, or any other policies or agreements relevant to site visitors/users.

APPTUTTi Right to Revise Policy

APPTUTTi reserves the right to change this policy at any time by notifying visitors/users of the existence and location of the new or revised privacy policy.  Any changes or updates to this policy will be effective immediately upon posting to the site.

Dispute Resolution

By accepting this policy, you agree that any disputes arising under this policy will be submitted promptly for binding resolution by an arbitrator with specific expertise in Internet and electronic communications matters and to be heard in Hong Kong Special Administrative Region.  APPTUTTi RESERVES THE RIGHT TO LIMIT SERVICE TO THOSE WHO ACCEPT THE TERMS OF THIS PRIVACY POLICY.  YOU EXPRESSLY CONSENT TO THE COLLECTION AND USE BY APPTUTTi OF PERSONALLY IDENTIFIABLE INFORMATION ACCORDING TO THE POLICY ABOVE.

Contact Us

For any question, claim or complaint that you may have with respect to the website, service or any content of APPTUTTi, please feel free to contact us via email: info@apptutti.com.
