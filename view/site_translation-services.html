Professional Translation Services

APPTUTTi provides professional translation services from English to Simplified Chinese. This allows for a more user-friendly experience for Chinese users and increases the compatibility of your apps with the local market. This service is only available to registered partners upon request. We offer free professional translation for the initial 1,000 words.

We offer this service to encourage all creators. We want the world’s largest mobile market to interact and love your creations. Through our professional services, one of the many barriers of entry into China is no longer a problem. 

PARTNERS RESPONSIBILITIES

Before using our translation service you must:

  • Complete the app submission
  • Provide the latest introductory materials for your app in English and in any document format
  • Submit the English language pack in a spreadsheet format for your app that requires translation

APPTUTTi SERVICE STEPS

  • Translate the partner’s language pack from English to Simplified Chinese
  • Translate each batch of 1,000 words within 48 hours
  • Upload the translated language pack to the partner resources portal and send an email to notify the partner once translation is complete
